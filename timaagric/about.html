Understanding Urban Farming

Urban farming is the process of growing food within urban areas, often using innovative, space-efficient methods like:

  • Hydroponics: Growing plants in water, without soil.
  • Aquaponics: Combining fish farming with plant farming in a single system.
  • Vertical Farming: Growing crops in stacked layers, often using artificial lighting.
  • Rooftop Gardens: Converting urban rooftops into productive green spaces.

About Urban Farming

Urban farming is reshaping the way we produce and consume food in cities. By using innovative methods like hydroponics and vertical farming, we can grow fresh produce closer to consumers while reducing our environmental impact. Join the movement today and be part of the change.
