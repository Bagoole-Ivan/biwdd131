Environmental Benefits

Urban farming reduces the carbon footprint of food production, improves urban air quality, and creates green spaces that support biodiversity.

Social Benefits

Urban farming strengthens communities, promotes food security, and provides educational opportunities for local residents.

Economic Benefits

It creates new jobs, supports local businesses, and reduces dependence on long-distance supply chains.
